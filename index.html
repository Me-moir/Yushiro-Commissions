Frequently Asked Questions

For more info, kindly reach on to us or check our privacy policy:

Is my account information safe?
Yes, we take security very seriously. All our pilots are vetted professionals, and we use secure methods to handle your account information. We never store your passwords.
What are your payment methods and accepted currency?
We accept Gcash, Paypal, and Maya for PHP currency, and Paypal only for USD currency. We also accept in-game currency for some services.
Do you accept downpayments?
For balances Php 200 and above, clients can make a down payment before the service starts.
Can I apply Send Money Protect?
Payments with 'Send Money Protect' are not accepted due to the lack of security features. Payments sent this way will be refunded. Only Family or Friends (FNF) payments are accepted via PayPal. Payments sent via other options are not accepted.
What is the policy on cancellations and refunds?
Refunds are not allowed once the pilot has started the service on the client's account. Pilots may cancel or reschedule services due to unforeseen circumstances, but alternative arrangements may be made with client agreement.
